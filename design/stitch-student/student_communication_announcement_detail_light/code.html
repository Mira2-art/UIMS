Guest Lecture by Dr. Alan Turing Jr.

We are profoundly honored to announce an upcoming guest lecture featuring the world-renowned computational theorist, Dr. Alan Turing Jr., a pioneer in the fields of Decentralized AI and Quantum Ethics.

In this exclusive session titled "The Ghost in the Ledger: Cognitive Sovereignty in the Age of Autonomy," Dr. Turing will explore the intersection of blockchain architecture and neural networks, challenging our current perceptions of machine agency and human responsibility.

This event is a mandatory attendance recommendation for final-year Computer Science and Philosophy majors, though it remains open to all members of the Trustech community. The lecture will conclude with a 30-minute interactive Q&A session followed by a light networking reception in the East Wing Terrace.
