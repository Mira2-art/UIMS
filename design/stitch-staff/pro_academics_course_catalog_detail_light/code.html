description Syllabus Summary

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the fundamental principles, algorithms, and applications of Artificial Intelligence. Students will explore classical AI techniques such as state-space search and logic-based systems, alongside modern approaches including deep learning and reinforcement learning.
