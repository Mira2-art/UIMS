WEEK 01-03

Dirac Notation and Matrix Mechanics

Review of Hilbert space, operators, and state vectors in abstract notation.

Angular Momentum & Spin

Addition of angular momenta, Clebsch-Gordan coefficients, and Pauli exclusion principle.

WEEK 08-12

Approximation Methods

WKB approximation, variational principles, and time-dependent perturbation theory.
