Academic Calendar Update: Semester II Final Examinations Schedule

Dr. Elena Rodriguez

Office of Academic Affairs • Oct 24, 2023

verified

Dear Faculty and Staff Members,

Following the recent Academic Board meeting, we are pleased to announce the finalized schedule for the Semester II Final Examinations. This year, we have implemented a new modular feedback system to streamline the grading process for our educators.

Please note the following critical dates for your departments:

  • Submission of finalized question papers: November 5th
  • Commencement of examination period: November 20th
  • Final grade entry deadline: December 15th

We understand the workload during this period and have arranged for additional administrative support across all departmental hubs. If you require specific technical assistance with the digital portal, please reach out to the IT Helpdesk via the "Quick Support" tab in your Staff Pro dashboard.

"Our priority remains the integrity of the assessment process while ensuring our staff members have the resources needed to provide quality feedback to our students."

Thank you for your continued dedication to Trustech Institute's academic excellence.
